Hero MotoCorp


One World One Hero 

As the world's largest manufacturer of motorcycles and scooters for 20 consecutive years, Hero MotoCorp knows what it takes to be a champion. With its unrelenting pursuit of its vision ‘Be the Future of Mobility’ and mission to ‘Create Collaborate Inspire’, the New Delhi (India)-headquartered Company has remained at the forefront of providing modern, technologically superior and eco-friendly mobility solutions to millions of customers around the world.

Led by Dr. Pawan Munjal, Chairman, Hero MotoCorp, the Company has grown into a global brand with presence in over 40 countries across Asia, Africa, South and Central America and the Middle-East. Hero MotoCorp’s manufacturing facilities are spread across three countries in two continents and it’s Research and Development (R&D) facilities are located in India and Germany. 

Hero MotoCorp recently achieved the milestone 100 million cumulative production landmark. The Company’s achievement of this milestone is one of the fastest globally. The roll-out of the 100 Millionth two-wheeler can be viewed here - https://100million.heromotocorp.com/ 

Hero MotoCorp has also set in action its plan to achieve the next 100 Million sales within the next decade – by 2030. The Company has an aggressive product plan for the next five years and during this time will launch 10 products every year.  

With cumulative sales of over 100 million motorcycles and scooters through more than 9,000 customer touchpoints across the globe, the Company continues to champion the aspirations of millions of people through its wide range of products and services.

Hero MotoCorp is also the preeminent leader in the Indian two-wheeler market, the world’s largest, with a motorcycle market share in excess of 50 per cent.

Technology at the Core

Like the global youth, Hero MotoCorp is aspirational, experimental, innovative, agile and enterprising. 

Achieving its aim of creating a global powerhouse of innovation and manufacturing excellence, the Company set up its first international technology centre, the Hero Tech Center Germany GmbH (TCG), on the outskirts of Munich in 2019. The new centre works in tandem with the company's global R&D hub, the Hero Centre of Innovation and Technology (CIT) located in Jaipur in the northern Indian state of Rajasthan, to develop new vehicle concepts and create mobility solutions of the future. 

Engineers, designers and product experts from over 10 countries are currently engaged at the CIT and TCG to develop future-ready products that suit the sensibilities of the Company's diverse global customer base.

The Company has also affirmed its commitment to promoting a sustainable future by making sizeable strategic investments in manufacturing electric vehicles and building an ecosystem for EVs. Hero MotoCorp has a three-pronged approach – one through its own internal project at CIT, the second is at Hero Hatch – its internal start-up and thirdly through its investment in Ather Energy. Ather is one of top technology start-ups in India that has launched a successful electric scooter and is also working on charging grids. Hero MotoCorp has over 35% equity stake on the Company. 

A Global Footprint

As a truly global enterprise, Hero MotoCorp has continued to expand its presence around the world by investing in new markets, introducing new products across segments and expanding its distribution networks. 
Over the past few years, Hero MotoCorp has rapidly augmented it’s global presence to be currently present in more than 40 countries across the globe. 

In September 2015 Hero MotoCorp commenced operations at its first overseas manufacturing facility in Colombia – thereby becoming the first Indian two-wheeler company to set up a manufacturing plant in Latin America.  

Hero MotoCorp’s second manufacturing facility at a global location is coming up in Bangladesh. This facility is expected to be operational towards the middle of this year. 

Hero MotoCorp has a strong focus to expand its business operations outside India and increase its presence in the existing global markets. The Company has a new slogan for its Global Business, which is R4 – Revitalize, Recalibrate, Revive and Revolutionize.

Hero MotoCorp has recently announced its foray into Mexico and will soon commence operations there. It has entered into a distribution agreement with Grupo Salinas. The two companies have come together to form one of the largest distribution networks globally to sell competitive, high-quality products in the large Mexican market.

Hero MotoCorp has also kept abreast of the global technology landscape by adopting technologies such as robotic process automation, artificial intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT). In key international markets, the Company now uses new age technology to approve loans in 10 minutes, thus differentiating itself through value-added customer experience. 

Redefining Sustainable Manufacturing

Hero MotoCorp remains committed to stay on the path of sustainable development. The Company's state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities maintain the highest ecological standards as do its world-class R&D facilities and the Global Parts Centre (GPC).

Sustainability is one of the key pillars of Hero MotoCorp’s operations. It continuously focuses on providing sustainable growth to the societies that it operates in and also to all stakeholders. 

Hero MotoCorp was recently figured in the prestigious Dow Jones Global Sustainability Index – the only two-wheeler company and one of the only 11 Indian companies to figure in the coveted list. The Company has set very ambitious targets in these areas - for example, to be 500% water positive by 2025, and 100% Carbon Neutral by 2030. 

In Safe Hands 

As a responsible organisation, Hero MotoCorp's efforts extend beyond business to contribute to the betterment of society. The Company's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) program is an essential part of its corporate strategy, as the Company has always believed in giving back to the society. 

CSR is not just an addition but also an intrinsic process in all the planning across the Brand. The Company’s CSR efforts have continuously brought about noticeable improvements in the lives of hundreds of thousands of people across the India. With a well-defined vision, all CSR activities are done under ‘Hero We Care’ which has the three broad parameters – Greener, Safer and Equitable. The Company encourages its employees and their families to serve as volunteers at many of its initiatives.

The Greener theme aims to get constructive ecological changes and fight the climate change and global warming.

The goal under the Safer theme is to make India’s roads safer and bring down the number of fatalities.

Hamari Pari, Educate to Empower (E²) and Enable are the Company’s programmes to empower girls/young women from the marginalized and underprivileged sections of the society.

During the Covid-19 pandemic in 2020, Hero MotoCorp contributed Rs. 100 Cr, along with other Hero Group companies, for COVID-19 relief efforts. It distributed 23 lakhs meals, 34,000+ ration kits, 37,201 litres of sanitizers, 44 lakhs face masks and 44,000+ PPE kits to the government hospitals, Police departments and other agencies. It developed and handed over 53 unique First Responder Vehicles (FRVs), custom-built as an upgrade on powerful Xtreme 200R motorcycle, to the government authorities in Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Mizoram, Jammu and Kashmir, Chhattisgarh, Himachal Pradesh and Orissa. 

Champion of Sports

Dr. Pawan Munjal firmly believes in the transformational power of sports on society and on its ability to transcend borders and connect people. In keeping with this belief, Hero MotoCorp has become one of the largest global corporate promoters of multiple sporting disciplines. The Company continues to be globally associated with Golf, Football, Field Hockey, Cricket and Motorsports. 

Strengthening ties in Golf

Hero MotoCorp has been an ardent supporter of Golf for over two decades now. The Company is currently a partner of all three prominent tours – the Asian Tour, the European Tour, and the US PGA. 

Moreover, Dr. Pawan Munjal and Hero MotoCorp share a close bond with golfing icon and one of the biggest sporting heroes of all time, Tiger Woods.  In 2014, Tiger Woods came on board as the Global Corporate Partner at Hero MotoCorp. It's a partnership that has grown from strength to strength  and which was renewed recently. 

Meanwhile, Hero MotoCorp's efforts in bringing innovation to Golf have borne fruit with the growth of the ‘Hero Challenge’ — an innovative one-hole knockout contest played under lights at three prominent European Tour events. The event is sought after by golfers and fans alike. 

Hero MotoCorp also hosts the exclusive ‘Hero World Challenge’ at Albany in the Bahamas, a PGA-sanctioned Tiger Woods invitational event, which continues to be a sought after invitational tournament. 

In addition, the Company has played a catalysing role in the growth of Golf in India. It has been the title sponsor of Hero Indian Open for men, which is co-sanctioned by the Asian Tour and European Tour, since 2006, and of the Hero Women’s Indian Open, which is a part of the Ladies European Tour, since 2010. Hero MotoCorp also sponsors India’s domestic Pro Tour for women. Many Indian men and women golfers are Hero Brand Ambassadors.

Taking Indian motorsport global
 
Hero MotoSports Team Rally, the rally-racing team of Hero MotoCorp, has become a flag-bearer of Indian Motorsport on the global arena. The Team has consistently delivered impressive performances across the world, including at the world’s most challenging Dakar Rally. 

Within just five years of its formation, the Hero MotoSports Team Rally has delivered race wins, a BAJA Championship, multiple podium finishes at reputed international rallies and finished the Dakar Rally twice inside Top-10. 

Growing Football in India

In its home market in India, Hero MotoCorp is the biggest corporate promoter of Soccer, supporting the game across all platforms. In addition to being the sponsor of the Indian national teams for men and women across age groups, Hero is also the title sponsor of all the major domestic leagues.  

At a global level, Hero MotoCorp is a partner of the popular Spanish Football team Atletico Madrid, one of the premier clubs in Europe. Argentine legend and current coach of Atletico Madrid Diego Simeone and popular Colombian defender Yerry Mina are Hero’s Brand Ambassadors. 

Popularising Field Hockey

Hero MotoCorp is committed to promoting and popularising Field Hockey in India and across the world. As a long-term ‘Global Leadership Partner’ of the International Hockey Federation (FIH), Hero MotoCorp supported the men’s Hockey World Cup held in the eastern Indian state of Odisha in 2018. 

Additionally, Hero MotoCorp was the Title Sponsor of Men’s Asia Cup 2017 and 2018 Asian Champions Trophy. 

Over the years, it has also partnered with major Hockey leagues held in India – Hero Hockey India League, Hero Hockey Champions Trophy, and Men’s Hockey World League.

Batting for Cricket

Hero MotoCorp has had a long association with Cricket in various capacities spanning over two decades. The Company is currently the Title Sponsor of the Hero Caribbean Premier League – the highly popular T20 Cricket tournament held annually in the Caribbean islands and the USA. Hero MotoCorp also supports leading international Cricket events across Cricket playing countries. 

Earlier, the Company was one of the Global Partners of the International Cricket Council (ICC). It was also the team sponsor of Delhi Daredevils and later, the Mumbai Indians teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL) T20 tournament and was also a partner of the IPL at the national level.

The world’s number one batsman and current captain of Indian National Cricket Team, Virat Kohli, is Hero’s Brand Ambassador, along with women cricketers like Smriti Mandhana. 

A Hero for All

Hero MotoCorp continues to sustain its leadership as the largest two-wheeler company in the world by providing innovative mobility solutions to its expanding base of customers around the world. In addition, it is setting new standards of sustainable manufacturing through its green initiatives, as well empowering people and creating an equitable world.
