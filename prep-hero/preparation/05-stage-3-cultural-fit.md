## üß† Behavioral Questions (STAR Method)

### 1. Conflict Resolution
**Q:** "Tell me about a time you disagreed with a Business Head."
**Context:** Hero is a traditional, powerful organization. Disagreement must be respectful but firm.
**S (Situation):** "At Amdocs, my VP wanted to promote a high-revenue manager who had multiple harassment complaints."
**T (Task):** "I had to protect the company culture without alienating the VP."
**A (Action):** "I didn't lead with 'No'. I led with data. I showed the attrition rate of that manager's team (40% vs 10% avg) and the hiring cost of replacing them. I demonstrated that promoting him would actually *cost* us more money in the long run. I proposed a compromise: A probationary period with mandatory coaching."
**R (Result):** "The VP agreed. The manager eventually left, but the team retention stabilized. I earned the VP's respect for speaking business language."

### 2. Adaptation / Agility
**Q:** "You are new to Auto. How will you cope?"
**Answer:** "HR is industry-agnostic, but *Context* is King.
1.  **Listen First:** My first 30 days will be 'Zero Opinion, 100% Listening'. I will visit the plants and dealerships.
2.  ** Leverage Peers:** I will lean on the existing HR team to understand the unwritten rules.
3.  **Fast Learner:** I completed my Power BI certification while working full time. I have the learning agility to master the auto-sector jargon in 3 months."
