## üìà Area 2: Talent Management & Succession (20% Weightage)

**The Question:** "How do you identify High Potentials (HiPos)?"
**The "Hero" Answer:**
"I use a **9-Box Grid** assessment, but valid data is key.
1.  **Performance** (Past 2 years ratings) on X-axis.
2.  **Potential** (Learning agility, aspiration, engagement) on Y-axis.
3.  **Calibration:** I facilitate calibration sessions with leadership to remove bias. Someone hitting sales targets isn't always a leader. We need to distinguish 'High Performers' from 'High Potentials'.
4.  **Action:** For HiPos, I deploy IDPs (Individual Development Plans) and rotation exposure. For Hero, this links directly to the **Ignite 2.0** pipeline."

## üöÄ Area 3: Capability Building & TNI (30% Weightage)

**The Question:** "Our sales team needs upskilling. How would you approach Training Need Identification (TNI)?"
**The "Hero" Answer:**
"I avoid the 'spray and pray' training approach.
1.  **Skill Gap Analysis:** I'd analyze the sales data. Are we failing at *opening* calls (lead gen) or *closing* deals?
2.  **Field Observation:** I believe in 'HR on the Ground'. I would travel with Area Sales Managers for a day to observe real gaps. Is it product knowledge? Or negotiation skills?
3.  **70-20-10 Model:**
    *   **10%:** Classroom training (Product features).
    *   **20%:** Mentoring (Shadowing a senior sales leader).
    *   **70%:** On-the-job projects (Assigning a tougher territory for a month)."

## ü§ù Area 4: Employee Relations & Culture (30% Weightage)

**The Question:** "How do you handle a Grievance from a high-performing but toxic manager?"
**The "Hero" Answer:**
"This is delicate. We cannot compromise culture for revenue.
1.  **Fact Find:** I would conduct a neutral investigation/conversations with the team.
2.  **Feedback:** I would have a crucial conversation with the manager. 'Your results are great, but your method is unsustainable. At Hero, we Create, Collaborate, and Inspire. You are creating, but failing to collaborate.'
3.  **PIP for Behavior:** I would put them on a Performance Improvement Plan specifically for *behavior*, not sales.
4.  **Decision:** If they don't shift, we exit. A toxic manager causes attrition of 10 other good employees, which costs more than one person's sales targets."
