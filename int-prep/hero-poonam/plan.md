## 4. Interview Stage Preparation

### Stage 1: Skill 1 Interview (HRBP & Business/Zonal Head)
*Focus: Core Competence & Business Acumen.*

*   **Likely Questions:**
    *   "Walk us through a time you implemented an organizational change. How did you handle resistance?"
    *   "How do you calculate Manpower Costs as a percentage of revenue?" (Check JD Section 3).
    *   "Describe your experience with Labor Laws and Compliance." (Crucial for manufacturing context).
    *   "How have you partnered with business leaders to drive profitability?"
*   **Tip:** Use the **STAR method** (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Quantify your results (e.g., "reduced attrition by X%", "improved engagement score by Y%").

### Stage 2: Specialized Presentation (Head HR)
**Topic:** *"Retention strategies for Sales & Marketing function at Hero MotoCorp"*

*   **Structure for the Presentation:**
    1.  **Context Setting:** Acknowledge the high-pressure nature of Sales/Marketing in the competitive Auto sector.
    2.  **Diagnosis (The "Why"):** Why do they leave? (Burnout, incentive structures, lack of growth, competitive poaching).
    3.  **Proposed Strategy (The "What"):**
        *   **Compensation:** Flexible incentive structures.
        *   **Carrers:** Clear path from "Territory Manager" to "Zonal Head".
        *   **Culture:** Recognition programs (e.g., "Sales Champion of the Month"), Work-life balance initiatives.
        *   **Capability:** Upskilling in digital sales tools.
    4.  **Implementation Plan:** 30-60-90 day rollout.
    5.  **Metrics:** How you will measure success (e.g., attrition rate < X%, Sales productivity).

### Stage 3: Final Skill Interview (HRBP for HR)
*Focus: Cultural Fit & Values.*

*   **Focus Areas:**
    *   Why Hero MotoCorp? (Connect with their "Future of Mobility" vision).
    *   Willingness to relocate to Gurugram (Confirm this explicitly).
    *   Handling conflict with senior leadership.
    *   Long-term career aspirations.
